Imagine you're trying to throw a ball into a basket with your eyes closed. You throw it, open your eyes, and see you missed by 2 meters to the left.

Your brain (the network) says, "Okay, next time, throw slightly more to the right."

In neural networks, this "miss distance" is the Error Rate. Backpropagation is just the messenger running backward through your brain cells (neurons) shouting, "Hey! Change your strength (Weight) a little bit so we don't miss next time!"
